About Us

At Batik Shop, we celebrate the timeless beauty of handcrafted batik. Our designs are inspired by tradition, created with care, and brought to life by skilled artisans. Each piece tells a story of culture, passion, and craftsmanship.

We are committed to preserving the art of batik and sharing its elegance with the world. From apparel to home decor, every creation is a masterpiece that blends tradition with contemporary style.
